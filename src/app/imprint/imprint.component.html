GDPR Compliance Statement

At Dr. Franke Datenwissenschaften OÜ, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of personal data following the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws. This GDPR Compliance Statement outlines our practices and policies regarding collecting, using, and protecting personal data.

1. Data Collection and Processing:

We collect and process personal data only for specified and lawful purposes. Any data collected is limited to what is necessary for the intended purpose, and individuals are informed about the processing of their data.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing:

We ensure that there is a lawful basis for processing personal data. This may include the necessity of processing for the performance of a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, protection of vital interests, consent, the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or the exercise of official authority, and legitimate interests pursued by the data controller or a third party.

3. Data Minimization:

We adhere to the data minimization principle by limiting personal data collection to what is adequate, relevant, and necessary for the intended purposes. We do not retain data longer than is necessary.

4. Data Accuracy:

We strive to maintain accurate and up-to-date personal data. Individuals have the right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete data concerning them.

5. Data Security:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of personal data. This includes protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

6. Individual Rights:

We respect the rights of individuals under the GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. We provide mechanisms for individuals to exercise their rights.

7. Lawful Data Transfers:

When transferring personal data to third countries or international organizations, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect the data, as the GDPR requires.
